Top 5 Python Performance Tips for 2026

1. Use list comprehensions

List comprehensions are faster than for loops for building lists.

2. Cache repeated computations

functools.lru_cache is your friend for expensive pure functions.

3. Profile before optimizing

Use cProfile to find the real bottlenecks.

4. Prefer generators for large data

Generators are lazy and memory-efficient.

5. Watch out for global lookups

Local variable access is faster than global lookups in tight loops.
